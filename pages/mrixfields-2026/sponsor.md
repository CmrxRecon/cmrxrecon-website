Founded on August 20, 2024, **Shanghai Point Imaging Healthcare Co., Ltd.** is headquartered in the **Grand NeoBay** science and innovation hub in Minhang District, Shanghai. The company’s core technology is derived from the commercialization of research achievements from the **National Engineering Research Center for Advanced Magnetic Resonance Diagnosis and Treatment Technology at Shanghai Jiao Tong University**. Powered by cutting-edge innovation and strong academic expertise, Point Imaging is committed to becoming a leading benchmark enterprise in the medical technology industry.

Point Imaging is dedicated to advancing the accessibility of magnetic resonance technology in primary healthcare and continuously improving the experience of both clinicians and patients, enabling more people to benefit from advanced medical imaging. Its flagship product, a **mobile low-field MRI system**, is designed to bring magnetic resonance imaging beyond conventional fixed facilities into a wider range of clinical settings, including **bedside care, emergency medicine, intensive care, and grassroots healthcare institutions**. With greater flexibility, lower infrastructure dependence, and stronger adaptability to diverse medical scenarios, the system supports the development of more inclusive, efficient, and patient-centered imaging services.
